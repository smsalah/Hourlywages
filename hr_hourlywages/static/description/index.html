Hourly Wages

Hourly Wages offers a simple solution for Hourly Payment in Payroll. This module does the following

  1. It Adds a field "hourlywage" in Employee Contract Form.
  2. It creats a Salary Rule "Hourly Salary" based on the said Filed and Finally
  3. It creats a Salary structure "Hourly Salary Structure"
